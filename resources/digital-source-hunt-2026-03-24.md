## HYMNALS

### Šamotulský kancionál (1561)
- **Status: NOT DIGITISED (full scans)**
- HTB indexes all 735 songs (metadata only, no full text). Knihopisné číslo: 12860.
- No scan found on Kramerius MZK, Kramerius NKP, Manuscriptorium, or Archive.org.
- Czech Wikiversity has structural description: [cs.wikiversity.org](https://cs.wikiversity.org/wiki/Piesně_Chval_Božských)
- e-Books on Demand (EOD) service via National Library may allow ordering a digital copy — **Michal to investigate**.
- Extremely rare physical copies. Czech Wikipedia lists surviving exemplars but no digital access.
- **Workaround:** Use HTB metadata for concordance matching; use Ivančický 1564 (revised from Šamotulský) as practical substitute.

### Ivančický kancionál (1564)
- **Status: PARTIALLY ACCESSIBLE**
- Kramerius MZK UUID `776edc45-4eb0-11e1-1457-001143e3f55c` — **needs browser verification; may require CZ library card**.
- **1572 edition** (Písně duchovní evangelistské, Ivančice press) digitised at [Manuscriptorium / Městské muzeum Polička](https://www.manuscriptorium.com/en/node/5216) — **illuminated, open access**.
- Mladějovská dissertation (MUNI 2008) catalogues all 745 songs with incipit, source, notation data: [is.muni.cz](https://is.muni.cz/th/lb9bi/)
- **Action:** Access the 1572 Polička digitisation via Manuscriptorium. Use Mladějovská catalogue for systematic concordance work.

### Prvý kancionál Jednoty (1501)
- **Status: NOT DIGITISED. Possibly LOST.**
- German Wikipedia notes: "Die 1501 wohl in Prag erschienene, verschollene geistliche Liedersammlung" — described as "verschollen" (lost/missing).
- No scan found anywhere. Text-only (no notation), extremely rare.
- **Note:** German Wikipedia also questions whether this was truly a Brethren hymnal or belonged to Utraquist circles.

### Rohov kancionál (1541)
- **Status: NOT DIGITISED (no scan found)**

### Kunvaldský kancionál (1576)
- **Status: LIKELY SAME AS 1576 IVANČICE REPRINT**
- The 1576 edition is the third printing of the Ivančice line (after 1561 Šamotuly, 1564 Ivančice). It reprints the Ivančice preface. Check HTB for precise content differences.

- **Status: CHECK KRAMERIUS VKOL**
- Annotation [96] gives a Kramerius VKOL (Vědecká knihovna Olomouc) link — **needs browser verification**.
