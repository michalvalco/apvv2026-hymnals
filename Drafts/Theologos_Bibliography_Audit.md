## A. Substantive issues (recommend addressing before submission)

### A.1. The Grešová-Sekelská 2023 title is incomplete — missing the Slovak portion of the bilingual title

**Location:** Bibliography, the second Grešová-Sekelská entry.

**Issue:** The current entry reads:

> GREŠOVÁ-SEKELSKÁ, A.: *Georgius Tranoscius Teschiniensis / Jiří Třanovský (1592–1637): Hymns from the Cithara Sanctorum (1636) – Selection*. Musicalia Istropolitana 4/2. Bratislava: Stimul, 2023.

External verification (academia.edu listing of the work) confirms the publication is bilingual, with the **Slovak title** preceding the English title. The full title is:

> *Georgius Tranoscius Teschiniensis / Jiří Třanovský (1592–1637): Duchovné piesne zo spevníka Cithara Sanctorum (1636) – výber / Hymns from the Cithara Sanctorum (1636) – Selection*

Our other bilingual/trilingual entries (Bodnárová's volume *Reformácia v strednej Európe / Reformáció Közép-Európában / Reformation in Mittel-Europa I*; the Kónya–Csepregi 2013 *Tri lutherské vyznania viery z Uhorska / Három lutheri hitvallás Magyarországon / Drei lutherische Glaubensbekenntnisse aus Ungarn*) preserve the full multi-language title. The Grešová-Sekelská 2023 entry should match this convention.

**Fix:** Update the entry to:

> GREŠOVÁ-SEKELSKÁ, A.: *Georgius Tranoscius Teschiniensis / Jiří Třanovský (1592–1637): Duchovné piesne zo spevníka Cithara Sanctorum (1636) – výber / Hymns from the Cithara Sanctorum (1636) – Selection*. Musicalia Istropolitana 4/2. Bratislava: Stimul, 2023.

Footnote 4 in the draft already uses the shorter English-only title — it should be updated to match for consistency. Alternatively, footnote 4 can keep the abbreviated form (footnotes typically permit shortened titles after first full citation), but in that case the bibliography full title is the authoritative reference.

**Recommendation:** Apply the fix to the bibliography. Leave footnote 4 in its current shortened form, since the bibliography now provides the full title for verification.

## B. Minor inconsistencies (fold into revision pass)

### B.1. Some draft footnotes use "(ed.).:" with a redundant period; the bibliography uses the cleaner "(ed.):"

**Locations:** Draft footnotes [^11] and [^12] use:

> CSEPREGI, Z.: Die Confessio Pentapolitana... In: KÓNYA, P. (ed.).: *„Nezameniteľné je dedičstvo otcov…"*

The "(ed.).:" form has both a closing period after the parenthesis and a colon. This is a typo-residue from the original drafting and should be the cleaner "(ed.):"

The bibliography correctly uses:

> ...In: KÓNYA, P. (ed.): *„Nezameniteľné je dedičstvo otcov…"*

**Fix:** Update footnotes 11 and 12 in the draft to use "(ed.):" — this is a 2-character delete (remove the redundant period). The Bodnárová footnote 28 already uses the correct "(eds.):" form, so the fix is needed only on the two Csepregi footnotes.

**Recommendation:** Apply during the British-English sweep, since it's a typographic correction that fits naturally into that pass.
