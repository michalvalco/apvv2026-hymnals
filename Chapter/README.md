## Relationship to the Theologos paper

The Theologos paper (`../Drafts/Theologos_Draft_v0.1_Full.md`) is a preliminary publication derived from one specific PAIR_006 finding. The chapter is the broader four-*loci* comparative study; the PAIR_006 finding becomes a case study in §5 (Transfer a recepcia). The paper and the chapter share evidence and phrasing where appropriate but differ in scope and target audience:

- **Theologos paper** — ~7,200 words; English; Reformation-history audience; one focused intra-codex argument.
- **Chapter** — book-chapter length (TBD by editor); Slovak; TEPALIT monograph audience; comprehensive four-*loci* comparative study with hymnological transfer case study.

## Source-base separation

The chapter draws on a much wider source base than the Theologos paper. To keep the two deliverables disciplined:

- **Verified peer-reviewed scholarship** is recorded in `../Sources/` with full bibliographic data, key arguments, quotes-with-page-numbers, and Michal's annotations. Both the chapter and the paper draw on these notes.
- **AI-generated research reports** (the six "Report" docs in the Monografia/Resources folder; the compass artifact; the Perplexity AC-1581 report) are scaffolding for orientation only. Per the project's citation discipline, every claim from these reports requires primary-source verification before it makes it into the chapter draft.
- **Primary sources** (Benka 2024 critical edition; the 229 PNG scans of AC 1581; CB 1575 Czech text; etc.) are the chapter's evidentiary base.
